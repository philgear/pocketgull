1. Overview

Pocket Gull ("we," "our," or "us") is a real-time medical Care Plan Strategy and AI consultation engine currently in Research & Development. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our application at pocketgull.app.

By using Pocket Gull, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the service.

R&D Status: Pocket Gull is an active research project. It is not a certified medical device and has not received FDA clearance or CE marking. All AI-generated outputs are for informational and research purposes only and must be verified by a licensed healthcare professional before any clinical action is taken.
Key principle: We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide clinical intelligence features. We never sell personal data, use it for advertising, or share it with unauthorized third parties.

3. Health Data (Google Health API)

When you choose to connect your Google Health / Fitbit account, we request access to the following data types via Google OAuth 2.0 with your explicit informed consent:

  • Resting Heart Rate — daily summary (bpm), last 30 days
  • Oxygen Saturation (SpO₂) — daily average (%), last 30 days
  • Sleep Duration & Efficiency — nightly summary (minutes), last 30 days
How health data is stored: Health biometric data retrieved from Google Health API is held in server memory only for the duration of your session. It is never written to a database, disk, or third-party storage. Tokens are ephemeral — they are cleared when the server restarts.

We do not access, collect, or store any health data types beyond those listed above and explicitly consented to. We will update this policy and re-request consent before adding new data types.

This integration complies with the Google Health API Developer and User Data Policy.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Provide biometric trend analysis within clinical intelligence dashboards
  • Support AI-assisted care plan optimization and consultation features
  • Maintain your session and authenticate your identity
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve application performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

We never use your data for: advertising, marketing, profiling for third-party purposes, re-identification after de-identification, or any purpose not described above.

5. AI Processing & Disclosure

AI-Generated Content: Clinical analyses displayed in Pocket Gull are generated by Google Gemini large language models. These outputs may contain inaccuracies, hallucinations, or incomplete information. They are clearly marked with an "AI-Generated" badge in the application interface.

When you initiate an AI analysis, the following data may be transmitted to Google's Gemini API:

  • Patient symptoms, conditions, and vitals as entered by the user
  • Medication lists and biomarker values
  • The selected analysis lens (e.g., Summary Overview, Nutrition, Precision Nutrients)

This data is processed under Google's Gemini API Terms of Service. Google does not use API-submitted data to train its models when accessed through the paid API tier.

Local AI Cache: AI responses may be cached locally on your device using AES-GCM encryption via the Web Crypto API. Cached data never leaves your device.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Google (AI services): Clinical queries may be processed by Google Gemini API to generate AI responses. We minimize personal identifiers in prompts.
  • Google Cloud: The application is hosted on Google Cloud Run. Infrastructure providers process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users or the public.

No health biometric data retrieved from Google Health API is shared with any third party other than as required for application hosting.

7. Data Retention

  • Health biometrics (Google Health API): In-memory only — cleared on server restart or when you disconnect your account.
  • OAuth tokens: In-memory only — not persisted to disk or database.
  • Audit logs: Retained for 90 days for security and compliance, then permanently deleted.
  • Patient records you enter: Retained until you delete them or close your account.

8. Security

We implement technical, administrative, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of health data:

  • All data is transmitted over TLS/HTTPS
  • OAuth 2.0 with PKCE (RFC 7636) on every authorization request
  • Patient data encrypted at rest using AES-256 (CryptoJS) in local storage
  • AI response cache encrypted with AES-GCM via Web Crypto API
  • Tokens stored in memory only — never written to disk
  • IP addresses are hashed (SHA-256) before logging
  • HTML output sanitized with DOMPurify to prevent XSS
  • Container images scanned with Trivy in CI/CD pipeline
  • Access to production systems is limited to authorized personnel
  • All health data access events are logged in an audit trail

To report a security vulnerability, email security@pocketgull.app.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Withdraw consent: Disconnect Google Health at any time via Integrations → Google Health → Disconnect.
  • Erase health data: Use "Disconnect & Erase Data" to permanently remove all synced biometric data from your session.
  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Object: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@pocketgull.app. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
