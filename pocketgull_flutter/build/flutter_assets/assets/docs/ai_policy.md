# Generative AI Prohibited Use Policy
**Last Modified: December 17, 2024**

Generative AI models can help you explore, learn, and create. We expect you to engage with them in a responsible, legal, and safe manner. The following restrictions apply to your interactions with generative AI in the Google products and services that refer to this policy.

## Prohibited Activities

Do not engage in dangerous or illegal activities, or otherwise violate applicable law or regulations. This includes generating or distributing content that:

*   **Child Safety**: Relates to child sexual abuse or exploitation.
*   **Violence & Extremism**: Facilitates violent extremism or terrorism.
*   **Intimate Imagery**: Facilitates non-consensual intimate imagery.
*   **Self-Harm**: Facilitates self-harm.
*   **Illegal Acts**: Facilitates illegal activities or violations of law -- for example, providing instructions for synthesizing or accessing illegal or regulated substances, goods, or services.
*   **Rights Infringement**: Violates the rights of others, including privacy and intellectual property rights -- for example, using personal data or biometrics without legally-required consent.
*   **Surveillance**: Tracks or monitors people without their consent.
*   **Automated Decisions**: Makes automated decisions that have a material detrimental impact on individual rights without human supervision in high-risk domains -- for example, in employment, healthcare, finance, legal, housing, insurance, or social welfare.

## Security & Abuse

Do not compromise the security of others’ or Google’s services. This includes generating or distributing content that facilitates:

*   **Malicious Content**: Spam, phishing, or malware.
*   **Service Disruption**: Abuse of, harm to, interference with, or disruption to Google’s or others’ infrastructure or services.
*   **Safety Bypass**: Circumvention of abuse protections or safety filters -- for example, manipulating the model to contravene our policies.

## Harmful Content

Do not engage in sexually explicit, violent, hateful, or harmful activities. This includes generating or distributing content that facilitates:

*   **Hate Speech**: Hatred or hate speech.
*   **Harassment**: Harassment, bullying, intimidation, abuse, or the insulting of others.
*   **Incitement**: Violence or the incitement of violence.
*   **Sexual Content**: Sexually explicit content -- for example, content created for the purpose of pornography or sexual gratification.

## Misinformation & Deception

Do not engage in misinformation, misrepresentation, or misleading activities. This includes:

*   **Fraud**: Frauds, scams, or other deceptive actions.
*   **Impersonation**: Impersonating an individual (living or dead) without explicit disclosure, in order to deceive.
*   **Sensitive Expertise**: Facilitating misleading claims of expertise or capability in sensitive areas -- for example in health, finance, government services, or the law, in order to deceive.
*   **Democratic Processes**: Facilitating misleading claims related to governmental or democratic processes or harmful health practices, in order to deceive.
*   **Human Attribution**: Misrepresenting the provenance of generated content by claiming it was created solely by a human, in order to deceive.

---

*We may make exceptions to these policies based on educational, documentary, scientific, or artistic considerations, or where harms are outweighed by substantial benefits to the public.*
