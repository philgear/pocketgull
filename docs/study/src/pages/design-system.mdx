# Pocket Gull — Design System
**Industrial Grace, Tactile Papercraft & Avian Personas**

---

## 1. Design Philosophy

Pocket Gull's visual language is governed by four founding principles:

| Principle | Origin | Expression |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **"Less, but better"** | Dieter Rams | Neutral backgrounds, monochromatic surfaces, elimination of non-functional decorations. Every pixel must earn its place. |
| **Origami Precision** | Japanese paper-folding | The origami seagull is constructed exclusively from flat polygons—folds imply depth, paper implies fragility and care. |
| **Papercraft Physics** | Tactile 3D Paper Art | Multi-layered paper card stock (`#F9F3D9`), sunflower yellow accents (`#F6B12B`), stacked drop shadows, and 3D unfold physics. |
| **Clinical Neutrality** | Laboratory instrumentation | UI chrome is deliberately silent so that the clinical data and AI output become the loudest elements on screen. |

> *"Good design is as little design as possible."* — Dieter Rams, Principle 10

## 3. The Gull Squadron — Avian Agent Personas

Every AI agent, orchestrator node, and UI moment in Pocket Gull has a name. They are all seagulls with signature physical props and custom SVG keyframe animations.

### 🔭 Gulliver — *The Overview Agent*
> *"I see the whole ocean from up here."*

- **Role**: Synthesizes the patient's full clinical picture — history, vitals, symptom matrix
- **Props**: Brass Telescope, Weathered Captain’s Logbook, Compass Rose
- **ADK Mapping**: `overview_agent`
- **SVG Animation**: `telescope-scan` — Lens sweep & horizon shimmer

---

### ⚡ Swoop — *The Interventions Agent*
> *"Spotted. Locked. Delivering."*

- **Role**: Handles actionable Care Plan recommendations — prescriptions, procedures, referrals
- **Props**: Leather Satchel, Stethoscope, Aviator Goggles
- **ADK Mapping**: `interventions_agent`
- **SVG Animation**: `satchel-bounce` — Satchel opening & green delivery pulse

---

### 🔦 Sentinel — *The Monitoring Agent*
> *"I never blink. I never look away."*

- **Role**: Keeps a watchful eye on recovery trajectory, vital trends, and outbreak alerts
- **Props**: Lighthouse Cap, Binoculars, Signal Lantern
- **ADK Mapping**: `monitoring_agent`
- **SVG Animation**: `lantern-beam-rotate` — 360-degree amber lighthouse beam sweep

---

### 📖 Scribes — *The Education Agent*
> *"Let me explain that in a way that actually helps."*

- **Role**: Translates Care Plans into accessible, patient-friendly formats & dyslexia exports
- **Props**: Reading Spectacles, Open Storybook, Ink Quill
- **ADK Mapping**: `education_agent`
- **SVG Animation**: `quill-write` — Ink quill writing stroke & page flip

---

### ⚡ Skimmer — *Flash AI Inference Backbone*
> *"Blink and you’ll miss me."*

- **Role**: Ultra-fast inference backbone powering streaming AI responses
- **Props**: Racing Goggles, Speed Lines, Mandible Probe
- **ADK Mapping**: `gemini-2.5-flash`
- **SVG Animation**: `speed-dash` — Cyan speed-trail pulse

---

### 🚨 Samaritan — *Good Samaritan Emergency Override*
> *"I don’t wait for Wi-Fi. Lives don’t wait."*

- **Role**: Offline emergency override — 110 BPM chest-compression metronome
- **Props**: Red Cross Armband, Defibrillator Paddle, CPR Metronome
- **ADK Mapping**: `offline_emergency_bypass`
- **SVG Animation**: `cpr-pulse-metronome` — 110 BPM heart pulse
