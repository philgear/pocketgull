## 2. Waitlist Intake Pipeline

### Developer / Early-Adopter Signups

* **Lead Gen Channels**: Functional medicine forums, LinkedIn medical AI groups, open-source repository watchers.

---

## 3. Onboarding Protocol
1. **Ethical Agreement**: Clinician accepts the doctor-patient confidentiality terms and verifies clinical credentials.
2. **KSS Baseline**: Establish typical circadian alertness rhythms for the custom fatigue tracking system.
3. **EHR Configuration**: Map patient data fields to the FHIR R4 Bundle exporter.
