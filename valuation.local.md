## 2. Key Valuation Drivers

Our $5.5M valuation cap is anchored on four premium architectural and market advantages:

1. **Active Reasoning Engine (Low Latency)**: PocketGull is built on Google Gemini’s Multimodal Live API, offering low-latency, bidirectional voice-based consult strategy. This is a significant competitive leap over traditional transcribers that only process text post-consult.
2. **Standardized Interoperability (EHR Native)**: Native export of simulated patient intake and consultant notes directly into FHIR R4 Bundle formats, dramatically reducing the friction of future integrations with Epic, Cerner, and Athenahealth.
3. **Interactive 3D Anatomy (Patient Engagement)**: The Three.js procedural skeletal and surface modeling engine gives practitioners a visual patient education toolkit, directly improving clinic retention and treatment compliance.
4. **Clinical Safety & Readiness (KSS Check)**: Built-in Karolinska Sleepiness Scale (KSS) and circadian cycle monitoring verify clinician alertness before consults, addressing emerging medical-legal compliance requirements for AI in healthcare.
