# PocketGull Clinical Validation Pilot Protocol
*Confidential - Internal Use Only*

This document defines the metrics and operational procedures for validating PocketGull in live clinical settings.

---

## 1. Study Goals & Hypotheses

* **Primary Hypothesis**: Using PocketGull's real-time care plan strategy companion reduces average chart documentation time by at least 60% compared to standard typing workflows.
* **Secondary Hypothesis**: Patients receiving interactive Three.js 3D care plan representations show a 30% higher treatment protocol compliance rate at 30-day follow-ups.

---

## 2. Validation Metrics

1. **Documentation Efficiency**:
   * *Time-to-Chart (TTC)*: Minutes spent completing clinical notes per patient encounter.
   * *After-Hours Charting (AHC)*: Total minutes spent charting outside normal clinic hours.
2. **Clinical Strategy Alignment**:
   * Comparison of AI-suggested dietary, supplement, and lifestyle adjunct protocols against manual expert practitioner plans to check strategy accuracy.
3. **Patient Engagement Score**:
   * Surveys measuring the patient's understanding of their anatomical/skeletal treatment paths.

---

## 3. Compliance & Security Safeguards

* **Data Minimization**: Raw audio inputs are streamed transiently for real-time analysis and are not saved.
* **Consent Protocol**: Patients sign a standardized disclaimer allowing real-time AI transcription analysis.
* **Bypass Gate**: At any point during a crisis, the clinician can click "Good Samaritan Mode" to bypass the consult engine, lock records, and start the chest-compression pacing metronome.
